About me

I'm Yubai Yuan, an assistant professor in Department of Statistics at Penn State Univeristy. I received my PhD degree in Department of Statistics at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2020, under the supervison of Annie Qu. Before coming to Penn State, I spent two years as a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics at UC Irvine.

My current research focus are:

  • Network Science: Complex network analysis, information diffusion, hypergraph modeling
  • Casual inference: Causal inference on graph, causal mediation analysis, applied causal inference for social science (e.g., peer effect)
  • Statistical Machine learning: optimal-transport-based data integration, active learning

Research opportunities
